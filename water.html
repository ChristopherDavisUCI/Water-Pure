Water Molecules

The following rules apply.

  1. Each atom of oxygen weighs 5.
  2. The weight of a hydrogen atom is always a positive even number.
  3. A molecule of water is made of one atom of oxygen and two atoms of hydrogen, and the atoms of hydrogen must have the same weight.
